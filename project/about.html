about skyeye

skyeye delivers accelerated intelligence by turning aerial imagery into structured insight for joint task forces.

purpose

skyeye enhances situational awareness for defense teams that monitor border zones, convoy routes, and field logistics.

use cases

teams rely on skyeye to validate reconnaissance, confirm movement of armored columns, and document compliance along ceasefire lines.

limitations

dense urban canyons, severe weather, and degraded sensor feeds may reduce detection scores and call for manual review.
